WHAT I DO

Me as a Teacher

I want to share with you my profound knowledge of Russian culture. The understanding of its soul is an enriching experience. The same goes for the French culture, on which I have a total crush.

I focus 50% of time on speaking activities. That way we will quickly learn to make small and natural dialogues.

My lessons are structured but easily adaptable to the student’s current needs or interests. I will be glad to work through your favorite French/Russian songs, books, articles, or films. I give all the materials and type up your mistakes and new words.
